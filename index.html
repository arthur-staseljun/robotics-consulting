Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from warehouse, logistics, and manufacturing teams considering automation.

How much does automation typically cost?

Costs vary widely — from a few thousand euros for software-based process automation to hundreds of thousands for physical robotics. That is exactly why an Automation Opportunity Assessment comes first.

Do we need robots or vendors already selected?

No. We can work with you from scratch, or alongside your existing vendor conversations. The assessment helps you evaluate options with clear criteria rather than vendor marketing.

Can you work with our existing systems and vendors?

Yes. We specialize in integration architecture — connecting new automation layers with existing WMS, ERP, and operational tools your team already uses.

What if automation turns out not to be viable?

We will tell you honestly. If the numbers do not support automation at this stage, you receive a clear explanation of why and what to revisit in 12–18 months. That clarity is worth the assessment cost on its own.

What industries do you focus on?

Primarily warehousing, fulfillment, logistics, and light manufacturing — industries where process efficiency and throughput directly affect profitability.
